Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) of your favorite spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 package (8 ounces) lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until browned. Drain the fat.
    3. Add the spaghetti sauce, diced tomatoes, water, salt, pepper, and basil to the skillet with the cooked beef. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
    4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    5. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
    7. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    8. Add a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture on top of the noodles.
    9. Repeat with another layer of meat sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese.
    10. Finish with a layer of meat sauce on top.
    11. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
    12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
